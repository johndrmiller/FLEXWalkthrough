Students can decorate their room by purchasing items from the Item Shop. As students progress through lessons and earn points, these points can be redeemed for decorative items from the shop. Purchased items appear in the Design My Room tab, and students can drag and drop items from the tab to their room to decorate and personalize it. New items become available every five lessons, and item categories include flooring, furniture, wallpaper, and miscellaneous items.
