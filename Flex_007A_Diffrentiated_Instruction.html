By selecting a student’s name, teachers can quickly and easily grade Differentiated Instruction lessons by choosing either “Successful” or “Unsuccessful“ from the dropdown.
In the Differentiated Instruction tab of the Digital Gradebook, teachers can see which students have Differentiated Instruction lessons that need to be scored, as well as previously scored lessons.
